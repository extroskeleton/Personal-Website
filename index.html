Bio

Joshua Bolton is a Kentucky-based graphic designer with an appreciation of eccentric designs and bold colors. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) at Eastern Kentucky University and is set to graduate in May of 2026. He has worked with the EKU College of Business and College of Letters, Arts, & Social Sciences to create social media campaigns, posters, and logos. In his free time, he enjoys exploring new interests and finding unique destinations to travel to.

Josh’s inspiration is drawn from the past to craft for the future. The visual aesthetic of the “early” internet serves as the groundwork to create vibrantly colored compositions that stand out from the increasingly monotone direction that design is shifting towards. Josh actively seeks clients who are looking for outside of the box thinking, vibrant brand imagery, and those looking to stand out.
